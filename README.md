🚀 SAP Procure-to-Pay (P2P) Cycle Project
📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates the end-to-end Procure-to-Pay (P2P) cycle in SAP Materials Management (MM).

The P2P process is a critical component of supply chain management, covering the complete flow from procurement of goods/services to vendor payment, while ensuring seamless integration with Inventory Management and Financial Accounting (FI).

🎯 Objective

To illustrate how SAP automates and streamlines procurement processes by:

Ensuring accurate procurement tracking
Maintaining efficient inventory management
Integrating purchasing with financial accounting
Reducing manual errors and delays
🏢 Business Scenario

A manufacturing company, ABC Pvt Ltd, procures raw materials from vendors for production.
SAP MM is used to manage procurement activities efficiently and ensure timely availability of materials.

🔄 Procure-to-Pay Process Flow
Purchase Requisition → Purchase Order → Goods Receipt → Invoice Verification → Payment
🧩 SAP Modules Used
SAP MM (Materials Management)
SAP FI (Financial Accounting)
📸 Process Steps
1️⃣ Purchase Requisition (ME51N)
Internal request to procure goods/services
2️⃣ Purchase Order (ME21N)
Formal order issued to the vendor
3️⃣ Goods Receipt (MIGO)
Materials received and inventory updated
4️⃣ Invoice Verification (MIRO)
Vendor invoice verified against PO and GR
5️⃣ Payment Processing (F-53 / F110)
Payment made to vendor
